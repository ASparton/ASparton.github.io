Combat DEMO 3D

Project type:

PC Game Demo

Team:

Solo project

Game engine & Languages:

Unreal Engine 4 | C++

Tools:

Git

Basic 3D melee combat system between a single player and an AI enemy. The player controls the main character and can run in all directions, pick up a weapon in the level and then attack the enemy. The player has the possibility to do a combo from 1 to 3 attacks. The enemy detects when the player is near and go close enough to him to attack him. Both characters have a life and die when its value is lower or equal to zero.

Camera & Movements

The player can make the character run forward, rotate, run backward and also jump. On top of that, he can control the camera at the same time and look around in all directions.

Melee combo

The player can do a simple melee attack as well as a combo to hit his opponent. The combo implies 3 different attacks with thier respective animations. To implement this feature, the main issue to solve to have a better user experience is to manage with accuracy the timing between each attack. I personally used the duration of the animations to know when to allow the player to use the attack input again.

See Greystone (player) class where the movements, camera and the combo are implemented

AI Skeleton Enemy

I designed a basic artificial intelligence that controls a character to face the player. The enemy has an idle position where he stands when no player is detected. The enemy can detect a player when he enters a certain detection area, and then run to his position. When the player enters another area close to the enemy, he is considered in the enemy's attack range, and he can be hurted. When the player gets out of the detection area of the enemy, the enemy goes back to his idle position.

See SkeletonEnemyController and SkeletonEnemy classes
